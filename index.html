ABOUT CONVENTION

The ISTE National Students’ Convention, hosted every year by one of the ISTE students’ chapter, is a forum for the exchange of scientific and technical innovative concepts and highlights the role of students in directing it towards excellence. It provides a conducive ambience for both student bodies and industries to discuss technical advancements and to propagate innovations towards further evolution.

   Venue : VIT University, Vellore, Tamil Nadu

   Date : 15th and 16th of October, 2016

Guest Lecture

Indian Society for Technical Education is a national, professional, non-profit making Society registered under the Societies Registration Act of 1860. First started in 1941 as the Association of Principals of Technical Institution(APTI), it was converted into "Indian Society for Technical Education" in 1968 with a view to enlarge its activities to advance the cause of technological education in the country. The major objective of the ISTE is to assist and contribute in the production and development of top quality professional engineers and technicians needed by the industries and other organisations.

Poster Presentation

Poster presentation is the presentation of research information on a poster. Participants can take part in the event either individually or in teams of three. Posters have to be submitted at iste@vit.ac.in on or before 12th October, 2016 and will be displayed for all to see. Based on the peering review of the abstract, few posters will be selected. For presenting the poster, speakers will be given 3 minutes.

Rules and Regulations
1. The poster should not be consisting of more than 100 words.
2. The poster should be of A2 or A3 size.
3. The poster should be mailed to iste@vit.ac.in before 12th October 16 with a subject: 19th ISTE National Students’ Convention – Poster Presentation.
4. The poster should not be greater than 15 mb.
5. The aspects considered in the competition are :Technical content of the poster , Presentation skills , Poster format and appearance.
6. Only jpgs are to be sent to the given email id.
7. Font to be used in the poster are Times New Roman or Calibri ONLY.
8. Your contact deatils should be mentioned in the bottom right corner of the poster.

Paper Presentation

Paper presentation is the oral summary of an accomplished research study of an empirical or theoretical nature. Participants can take part in the event either individually or in teams of two. Papers have to be submitted at iste@vit.ac.in on or before 8th October, 2016. Based on the peering review of the abstract, few papers will be selected for presentation. For presenting the paper, speakers will be given 8 minutes to present followed by a 2 minutes question answer round.

Rules and Regulations
1. Maximum number of authors is 2.
2. Abstract should not exceed 1 page and paper should not exceed 15 pages.
3. The subject of the mail with the attached abstract and later, the final paper should be ‘19th ISTE National Students’ Convention – Paper Presentation’.
4. Bring your college Identity-Card & receipt on event day.
5. Please bring your PowerPoint presentation on a CD and on a pen-drive.
6. Soft-copies of the submitted paper and PowerPoint presentations must be in the .docx/pdf format.
7. Please Mail your abstract, paper and ppt to: iste@vit.ac.in
8. Please bring 2 hardcopies of the submitted paper on the day of the event.
9. The teams will get 8 minutes for presentation followed by question and answer session.
10. Persons from different institutions can be a part of the same team. However, one person may not be a part of multiple teams for the same event.
11. The mail with submissions should contain:

Title- theme of the paper.
Name of both the authors.
Phone no of both the authors.
E- Mail ID :

12. Paper should be in a one column format and should not exceed 15 pages.
13. Paper should be in Times New Roman font of size 12.
14. For headings use bold and a font of size 16.
15. Paper must contain Introduction (abstract), Materials and Methods / Flow Chart, Result and Discussion, Conclusion and References.
16. Paper must contain index, list of figures, list of tables, abstract, introduction, point wise description of subject and conclusion and references.
17. Paper must be preceded by a cover page specifying the title of the paper, names of authors and their college names as also their contact numbers and email ids.
18. Last day to submit soft copy of your report (via e-mail) is: 5th October, 2016.
19. After you have submitted the soft copy, a panel of judges will go through your material and if you are shortlisted to give the presentation, you will be notified via email.
20. The abstract mail should contain abstract, references, your e-mail id and mobile number.
21. The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.

Prize Money worth INR 10,000

Registration Fee per Paper: INR 100

CAD Modelling

Computer Aided Design is mainly used for detailed engineering of 3D models and 2D drawings of physical components, but it is also used throughout the engineering process from conceptual design and layout of products.
The participants will be given a theme on the spot and will be given 3 hours to create a model from scratch. SolidWorks and AutoCAD are the two softwares that can be used for modelling. Use of internet during the event will not be allowed. The developing and designing will be followed by a 2 minute presentation explaining various features of the model. The participant with the most innovative model/design will be awarded as winner.
